Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Pidgeon

  1. Pidgeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pidgeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pidgeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About Cicada

- Cicadas are insects.
- Cicada nymphs fall down and live underground.
- Cicada nymphs can survive very high falls.
